What sets City as a Platform apart

O que distingue o City as a Platform?

Six practical strengths municipalities feel in week one — plus alignment with Portugal’s national strategy for smart territories (ENTI).

📊

Real-time data

Dashboards with minute-by-minute updates so leaders and operators can decide with current conditions—not last month’s export.

🔗

External platforms

Aggregates data from many sources for a single, holistic view of the territory instead of disconnected silos.

🧩

No-code / Low-code

Teams can create and evolve their own apps with a no-code and low-code approach—faster delivery, more agility.

📱

Mobile-friendly

Responsive layouts so field teams and citizens keep access to critical maps and workflows on phones and tablets.

✨

Easy to use

Designed for city administrators and citizens alike—reducing friction and avoiding “only the vendor can change this”.

🇵🇹

Aligned with ENTI

Supports the National Strategy for Intelligent Territories: interoperable, sustainable, citizen-centred digital transformation.

Estratégia Nacional para os Territórios Inteligentes (ENTI)

A plataforma está alinhada com a ENTI, promovendo a transformação digital dos municípios com soluções interoperáveis, sustentáveis e focadas no cidadão.
