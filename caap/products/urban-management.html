Urban management

Deliver location map issuance and claims confrontation services against development plans on intranet and/or internet — templates, information layers, process queries, and online plan availability.

Location map issuance

The municipality provides an application for issuing location maps online — publicly or internally.

  • Issue a single map or a collection for process documentation, with predefined scales and sheet formats
  • Archive, search, and print issued maps
  • Select or draw a location on the map
  • Search by address, POI, parishes, spatial classes, cadastral matrices, or portal data
  • Collect applicant data via forms stored on the platform
  • Back office for application configuration

Claims confrontation

Land management and territorial planning tools for municipal technicians.

  • Issue confrontation maps for process documentation
  • Select or draw the claim on the map
  • Create maps for printing
  • Occupancy matrices by spatial class with areas, percentages, and regulations
  • Search toponymy, addresses, POIs, parishes, spatial classes, cadastre
  • Consult regulations and planning instrument legislation
  • Integrate national and regional plans for joint analysis
  • Back office configuration
