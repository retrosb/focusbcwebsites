Platform

City operations in four modules

CAAP is built as a system: each part connects to the same map and the same operational records.

Modules

Map & territory

Layers, assets, and spatial data in one workspace.

Mapify · App Builder

Operations & workflows

Tasks, inspections, incidents, and approvals.

Incident management · Urban hygiene · Traffic restrictions

Data integration

APIs and connectors to external systems and open data.

Connectors and APIs keep asset and work records in sync with tools you already run.

Dashboards & reporting

KPIs, monitoring, and exports for management and compliance.

Built on the same tasks and metrics your teams create in daily operations—not a separate reporting silo.

Architecture (overview)

Data → platform → operations → decisions

Data lands in the platform, flows into workflows and apps, and rolls up to dashboards your teams already use.
