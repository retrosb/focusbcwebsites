Manutenção dos Espaços Públicos

Incident management, infrastructure repair, urban hygiene, dashboards, and operations-centre views — aligned with the 2026 solution map.

This domain bundles operational visibility for teams that keep streets, equipment, and public assets in good condition. Typical building blocks include incident management, hygiene planning, and Mapify-driven dashboards fed by sensors and work orders.

Design note (PDF): use local photography from client municipalities, icons, and a client map for emotional, human-centred storytelling rather than a flat SaaS look.
