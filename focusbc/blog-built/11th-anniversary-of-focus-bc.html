11th Anniversary of Focus BC

focus-bc.com/post/11th-anniversary-of-focus-bc



Last Monday, Focus BC turned 11 years old. We invited everyone to come to the office for some cake and champagne. This year's cake was special since it includes every single member of the team, with our fresh new photos.

In the evening we went out for dinner at Maat Café & Kitchen near the Tejo river. Pretty fancy dinner, don't you think?



We asked the Managing Partners, Nuno Serra, Sandro Batista and Vasco Pinheiro, to say a few words about this company milestone.



Let's work hard in 2023 and build more memories together 🎉🥂
