Cidades Inteligentes by Google Cloud ends in Cascais

focus-bc.com/post/cidades-inteligentes-by-google-cloud-ends-in-cascais

After 4 incredible events in Faro, Coimbra, Famalicão and Cascais, Cidades Inteligentes by Google Cloud says goodbye. It was a pleasure to have been part of this ambitious initiative by Google Portugal.



Cascais welcomed us on May 25 with a sunny day and many City as a Platform clients - Lisboa, Seixal, Estrela and EMEL. The presentations went as planned and our Managing Partner, Vasco Pinheiro, had the opportunity to present City as a Platform, our platform for urban intelligence dedicated to managing territories such as cities or towns in their multiple dynamics and perspectives.
