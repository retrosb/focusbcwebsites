Focus BC at Google Cloud Day Lisbon ’25: AI for Data, Cities &amp; Infrastructure

focus-bc.com/post/focus-bc-at-google-cloud-day-lisbon-25

June featured a full calendar of industry events, with Google Cloud Day Lisbon ’25 held on June 26, 2025, at the iconic Convento do Beato in Lisbon.


Focus BC joined technology leaders and cloud experts at Google Cloud Day Lisbon ’25 for a day of real-world AI showcases, product insights, and deep dives into how cloud technologies are transforming data, infrastructure, and urban systems.


Why It Mattered?

Google Cloud Day Lisbon, is the largest Google event in Portugal, served as a hub for innovation and knowledge-sharing. Attendees gained:


  • First-hand access to the latest Google Cloud product updates

  • Live demonstrations and technical sessions

  • Success stories from clients and partners across industries

  • Interactive talks with tech leaders and decision-makers

  • Networking with national and international professionals



Strengthening Our Mission

Focus BC has been part of this event’s journey since 2023, with a strong presence in previous editions through  Mapify, our no-code platform to process real-time geospatial data, focused on location intelligence. This time we returned as Focus BC, reinforcing our mission to deliver solutions for smart cities and sports event management.


Thank you to everyone who stopped by our booth, whether you came to explore City as a Platform, dive into Mapify, experience Virtual Venue, or simply share a chat with the team. Your curiosity and energy made the day.


And yes, we kept the tradition alive. Another Cloud Day meant another team photo with Sofia Marta and Daniel Cruz. Some moments don’t need reinventing!




See you at the next edition!
