Focus BC is coming to Portugal Smart Cities Summit 🏡⁣🏥🏦🚖

focus-bc.com/post/focus-bc-is-coming-to-portugal-smart-cities-summit

For the second year in a row, Focus BC will have its own booth at Portugal Smart Cities Summit, the biggest smart cities event in Portugal. As a pioneer company in smart cities nationally, we have to be where the action is and we're very excited to meet new partners and showcase City as a Platform to more people at the event.


City as a Platform allows cities and councils to manage, visualise and plan their territory and public services in a connected and intelligent way. Some of our clients include Junta de Freguesia de Estrela and Câmara do Seixal. Here's what they have to say about City as a Platform:





At our booth, you'll have the chance to try City as a Platform yourself and meet our fantastic team who will be there to discuss how we can help tackle your territory's problems.

Portugal Smart Citites Summit is happening from October 11th to 13th at FIL - Parque das Nações.


But, why wait until Portugal Smart Cities Summit to talk about making your city smarter? Explore City as a Platform's website and get in touch with us today!
