Focus BC receives PME Excelência 2021 award

focus-bc.com/post/focus-bc-receives-pme-excelência-2021-award



Focus BC was distinguished with the status of PME Excelência 2021 (SME Excellence 2021) award. These companies are selected by IAPMEI and Turismo de Portugal annually. This award is given to the PME Líder companies with the best performance.


Nuno Serra, Focus BC Managing Partner, at the PME Excelência ceremony in Batalha.


See the article "Focus Bc received PME Líder status once again in 2021".


"This distinction is the result of the path of rigor and sustainability that has been followed since the foundation of the company, and to which we are all committed. It positions us as a reference company in the Portuguese business scene, demonstrating the excellence of our performance, our solidity, and resilience, as well as our ability to adapt and manage in a context of high uncertainty. This distinction also demonstrates our credibility and the trust that our customers and partners feel when working with us." shared Nuno Serra, Managing Partner at Focus BC.
