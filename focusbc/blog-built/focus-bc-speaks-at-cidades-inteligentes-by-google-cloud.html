Focus BC speaks at Cidades Inteligentes by Google Cloud

focus-bc.com/post/focus-bc-speaks-at-cidades-inteligentes-by-google-cloud


In order to present transformative solutions to Portuguese cities, Google Cloud reached out to us and Devoteam G Cloud to start a multi-city event named Cidades Inteligentes by Google Cloud (“Smart Cities” by Google Cloud).


The event kicked off in Faro, on March 24th. Our Managing Partner, Vasco Pinheiro, was there to present City as a Platform, our product for Google Urban Intelligence with the goal of supporting Municipal Councils on a path to become more digital and sustainable.


Speakers at Cidades Inteligentes by Google Cloud


City as a Platform gives cities full control and visualization over their territorial planning and services management across its public services and departments. It provides the possibility to extend the reach and involve its own citizens with more transparency.



The best way to understand more about City as a Platform is to explore our case studies regarding the Municipal Councils of Lisboa, Lagos and Estrela, as well as companies such as Infralobo and GIRA.


Come meet us at the next Cidades Inteligentes events, all over Portugal.
