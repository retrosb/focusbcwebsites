Focus BC wins Google Cloud Solution Partner of the Year 2022

focus-bc.com/post/focus-bc-wins-google-cloud-solution-partner-of-the-year-2022

As official Google Partners, Focus BC was invited to participate in the Google Cloud Christmas Partner Meetup 🎄 in Teatro Dom Luiz Filipe (Teatro da Luz). It was an amazing opportunity to talk with more Googlers and meet new potential partners.



We received the Solution Partner of the Year award for our work with City as a Platform, and Mapify IoT.


From left to right: Daniel Cruz (Google), Luís João (Google), Vasco Pinheiro (Focus BC), Sandro Batista (Focus BC) and Isaac Hernández (Google)



City as a Platform Incident Manager



New York City Mapify demo


After having an intense smart cities roadshow with Google with stops at Faro, Coimbra, Famalicão, and Cascais it's great to end the year like this.


Thanks Google!
