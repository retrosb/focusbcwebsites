Virtual Venue and Arena Events Partnership For Sports Competitions

focus-bc.com/post/virtual-venue-and-arena-events-partnership-for-sports-competitions

We are thrilled to announce our partnership with Arena Events+Venues to deliver cutting-edge event planning and venue operation services. Building on a strong, existing collaboration, this new partnership combines Arena Events+Venues' operational expertise with Virtual Venue's innovative software platform to revolutionize how prestigious competitions are planned and executed.


Virtual Venue is our advanced venue management platform software for seamless event planning and real-time monitoring, optimizing stadium and event operations. It brings everything together in one place so teams can collaborate easily, make real-time updates, and ensure every detail is covered ahead of the event.


“We are excited to partner with Focus BC as the official collaborator for managing and executing operational planning, alongside other advanced tools, on the Virtual Venue platform. With our extensive expertise in organizing world-class sports events, combined with the innovative capabilities of the Virtual Venue interface, we are poised to bring transformative change to the industry.” André Frota, Arena EV's Sports Architecture Lead for Middle East, Europe, and North America.

Our first project together kicks off with a commitment to support two flagship events: the CONCACAF Gold Cup and the CONCACAF Nations League. In the initial phase, the Virtual Venue platform will be utilized primarily by the Arena Events+Venues to gather all the relevant information about the venues and event details. Over time, CONCACAF members will also gain direct access to the platform, empowering them to consult and edit all relevant information through seamless, real-time online collaboration.




We are proud to collaborate with Arena Events+Venues to set new benchmarks for innovation and excellence in the sports industry. Together, we aim to elevate the experience for everyone involved, from the organizers and teams to the players and fans.
