Infralobo Case Study - Smart Resort Management

focus-bc.com/post/infralobo-smart-resort



"We are talking about the concept of territory management with the use of the most modern communication and information technologies available. There is already talk much on the concept of smart cities, but there are few cases of its application in practice. The fact that Infralobo is now working at the level of a Smart Resort, means that we are giving the example.” - Vítor Aleixo, Mayor of Loulé.


Customer


Infralobo is a company founded in 1999 that provides quality operational management, maintenance and cleaning services to the luxury resort of Vale do Lobo. Infralobo is a municipal company belonging to the Municipality of Loulé.







Challenge


Focusing on a smart city strategy, Infralobo intended to innovate by creating the world's first Smart Resort, leveraging a dedicated location-based platform supported by modern information and communication technologies, in which different services across a large urban area could be efficiently monitored and managed by a small team.




Results

  • Increases efficiency

Over 90% of incidents completed every month

  • Promotion of public participation

Around 193,000 total accesses and almost 400 registered users

  • Optimization of material and human resources

  • Reduction of costs
