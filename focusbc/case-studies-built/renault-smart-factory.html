Groupe Renault Case Study - Smart Factory Incident Management

focus-bc.com/post/renault-smart-factory





Focus BC developed the Integrated Incident Management System to monitor and manage incidents on the factory floor.


Customer


Groupe Renault is a French multinational car manufacturer. Groupe Renault markets are based on five brands worldwide: Renault, Dacia, Renault Samsung motors, Alpine and Lada.


In Portugal, the group has a factory in Cacia, municipality of Aveiro. The Cacia factory has been producing parts and components for the automotive industry since September 1981.






Challenge


Groupe Renault wanted a solution to manage and improve the process of incident identification, tracking and resolution, as well as nonconformities detection and reporting in secure areas of the factory.




Results

  • This system made it possible for all employees to register events to help reduce risks and improve the working environment;

  • Ease of use by employees;

  • Automatization of event management processes;

  • Measurement of service levels and high standards of quality
