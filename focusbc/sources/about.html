Who we are

We build software for operational teams, not for presentations

Focus BC develops platforms for teams that need to understand what is happening, coordinate across people and systems, and act with confidence.

Our work is focused on two demanding environments: municipalities and global sports events. In both cases, operations depend on timely information, shared visibility, and software that can support real-world decisions.

We build products designed to evolve over time, support repeated use, and stay coherent as needs grow more complex.
